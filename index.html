Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?

No. These classes and workshops are beginner-friendly.

What do I take home?

You’ll leave with a finished piece of silver jewelry that you created and polished yourself.

Where can I buy Barbara’s work?

Visit Melita’s Home in Ossining or Barbara’s Etsy store: bkirbyjewelry.etsy.com.

Can I book a party?

Yes — we offer group events. All supplies included.

Are refreshments included?

Yes, light snacks and drinks are provided.
