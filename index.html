Welcome to B Kirby Jewelry

Explore your creativity and make your own one-of-a-kind jewelry in a fun, supportive environment. Barbara Kirby offers hands-on metal clay classes, private parties, and creative workshops for all skill levels.

Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, our classes provide the perfect opportunity to learn new techniques and create beautiful pieces you'll be proud to wear or give as gifts.

Metal Clay Jewelry Classes

Learn to make beautiful, wearable art with metal clay. Our classes are designed to be fun, beginner-friendly, and full of creativity.

In our classes, you'll learn:

  • Basic metal clay handling and shaping
  • Texturing and design techniques
  • Firing and finishing methods
  • Setting stones and creating unique pieces

Meet Barbara

Barbara Kirby started making jewelry as a way to heal—and discovered a lifelong passion. She now teaches others the joy of turning raw materials into radiant, wearable art. With years of experience in metalsmithing and metal clay, Barbara's workshops are both educational and inspiring.

"I believe everyone has creative potential waiting to be unlocked," says Barbara. "There's something magical about watching someone create their first piece of jewelry and seeing their face light up with pride and accomplishment."

Barbara is a certified PMC (Precious Metal Clay) instructor and has been teaching jewelry-making for over 15 years. Her work has been featured in local galleries and art shows throughout the Hudson Valley region.
