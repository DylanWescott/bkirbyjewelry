About Barbara Kirby

Barbara Kirby is a Hudson Valley-based jewelry artist and educator specializing in silver metal clay. With over 15 years of experience, she’s taught hundreds of students how to create stunning, meaningful jewelry pieces. Her approach is warm, encouraging, and highly personalized—perfect for beginners and experienced makers alike.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have experience?

No! All workshops and classes are beginner-friendly. Barbara walks you through every step.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing. You’ll be working with small tools and may get a little dusty.

Will I really finish a piece of jewelry?

Yes! You’ll leave with a beautiful, kiln-fired silver piece—ready to wear or give as a gift.

What if I want to buy Barbara’s work?

Barbara sells her jewelry at Melita’s Home in Ossining and online at her Etsy shop.

Where are classes and workshops held?

Workshops are hosted at Melita’s Home, and private classes can be arranged at local venues or your own home.

What if I can’t make it after registering?

Contact Barbara at least 72 hours in advance for deposit credit toward a future workshop.

How many people can attend a workshop?

Workshops are limited to 8 participants so everyone gets personal attention.

What if I want to host a party?

Great idea! Contact Barbara to schedule. All supplies are brought to you, and every guest makes their own silver creation.
