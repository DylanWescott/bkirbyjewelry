About Barbara Kirby

Barbara Kirby is a metal clay jewelry artist with over 15 years of experience in creating, teaching, and inspiring others through the art of wearable silver sculpture. Her work has been exhibited throughout the Hudson Valley.

Yes! Barbara’s work is featured at Melita’s Home in Ossining. You can also browse available pieces on her Etsy shop.

No experience needed—our workshops are beginner-friendly and all materials are provided.

You’ll get a confirmation email and a reminder before the event. The remaining balance is paid at the workshop.
