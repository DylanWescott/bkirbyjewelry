Meet Barbara

Barbara Kirby started making jewelry as a way to heal—and discovered a lifelong passion. She now teaches others the joy of turning raw materials into radiant, wearable art. With years of experience in metalsmithing and metal clay, Barbara’s workshops are welcoming, hands-on, and filled with encouragement.
